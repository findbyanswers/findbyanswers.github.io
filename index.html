How it works

With Find by Answers you anonymously ask and answer questions.

We aggregate data and let you find people according to answers you and other users gave.

You may chat with people we found for you.

Go!

Find by Answers is useful

Find dates or friends

Study people opinions

in various aspects

Features

  • Register anonymously
  • Ask questions
  • Answer questions
  • Find people who gave same answers as you did
  • Find people who gave particular answers
  • Chat with people you found
  • View sophisticated statistical reports:
    • Most popular questions
    • Most unique and most alike users
    • Multi-dimensional statistics on answers
  • And more...

Your help is needed

Find by Answers is an open source and open business project.

We share everything.

We need all types of contributors:

  • UI / UX / Web / Graphic designers
  • Marketing people
  • Lawyers
  • Mobile / Back-end / Web Developers
  • Quality Assurance people
  • Beta users

And of course we need money donators and investors.

Why contribute?

  • Give something to society for free
  • Acquire experience and improve your skills
  • Extend your resume with an open source contribution
  • Show your skills to the World and grab some fame and glory
  • Earn some money once we start paying for contributions
  • Leave a mark on history
  • Your own reasons...

So if you feel that you can help building a great tool that will change the world, then you are welcome for contributions!
